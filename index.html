About Axiom

Built from years of watching feedback go unused

I'm William Christner, founder of Axiom. After years on IT contracts across different companies, I kept seeing the same pattern: employees had ideas, frustrations, and solutions—but their voices rarely made it to leadership, and survey results vanished into Excel.

I started Axiomlose that gap. Our mission is simple: make it easy for people to speak honestly, and make it just as easy for leaders to act on what they hear—with clear insights, practical recommendations, and follow-up tracking to see if changes work.

"When employees are heard, leaders succeed."
